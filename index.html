How to add an Accessible AI Assisted Summary

  1. Use the Accessible Summary AI Assistant to prepare all the content you need, including a short description and a full summary written in clear, accessible language. The assistant is hosted in DAIsy, so you will need to sign in with your Imperial SSO to use it.
  2. Use the Submission Form to complete the form with your prepared content, and upload a suitable image together with the requested project details.
  3. Submit the form. Once your submission has been processed, it will appear on this website for others to view.
